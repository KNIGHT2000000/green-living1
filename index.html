About Us

Green Living Academy is a sanctuary of knowledge dedicated to fostering sustainability and combating climate change. Founded in 2025, we empower individuals with innovative tools and education to create a greener tomorrow.

Our Aim

Our mission is to inspire a global movement towards sustainable living, equipping people with the understanding to protect our planet's natural resources for future generations.

The Impact

Lack of awareness on sustainability and climate change drives deforestation, pollution, and biodiversity loss, threatening ecosystems and human well-being. This ignorance fuels a cycle of environmental degradation and social inequity.

Our Solution

We address this through interactive learning modules, simulations, and expert-led content, making sustainability accessible and engaging for all, fostering a community committed to change.
